# The Corrupted Virtues — World & Lore Bible

> **Source of truth for the world & setting.** Living document — edit freely.
> Distilled from the D&D-origin campaign and design docs; **deliberately simplified for
> the game** (the tabletop campaign had far more detail than the game needs).
> _Last reviewed: 2026-05-18._

## Origin

Began as a **D&D homebrew campaign** by Joey Schlote (Moral Support Studios), now adapted
into a tactical RPG. **The original tabletop player-characters are NOT used in the game.**
Detailed tabletop story beats are intentionally trimmed — the game keeps the *world,
themes, and bosses*, not the session-by-session plot.

## The World of Areti

A peaceful realm whose stability comes from **seven regions supporting one another**.

- At its center stands **Paradeisos**, a great tower reaching to the heavens, home of
  **The First King of Areti** — the bridge between the divine and the mortal.
- Each of the seven lands is ruled by a divine sovereign — **a Virtue**.
- **The Choir**: seven Archangels, one bonded to each Virtue.
- **Guardians**: village-level guardian angels. **The player is a Guardian** from a
  remote village who rises to purge the corruption consuming Areti.

When a Virtue falls to its paired **Sin**, it becomes a corrupted **Great Beast** and its
region decays. The Guardian travels region to region, **purifying** each Virtue (not
killing them — they are restored and survive), then confronts the source itself.

**Boss creature design is open.** The tabletop bosses were chosen under D&D 5E monster
constraints (Unicorn, Drider, Copper Dragon, T-Rex, Glabrezu, Aboleth, Gynosphinx).
For the game these are **inspiration only** — each corrupted-Virtue Great Beast should be
an original creature that fits the *vibe of its Virtue, Sin, element, and region*.

## Elements & the Corruption Theme

Seven elements: **Nature, Water, Earth, Fire, Energy, Dark, Light**.

Relationships (from the combat code):
- Binary pair: **Light ⇄ Dark**
- Cycle: **Water → Fire → Nature → Earth → Energy → Water**

**Locked theme:**
- **Dark = Corruption.** **Light = Virtue / Purity.**
- "Corrupted" is the **enemy / world state**, not a player-accrued resource.
- Boss fights are won by depleting a **Corruption gauge** — *purify, not kill*. The
  Virtue is restored and aids the realm.
- The 7-element matchup chart remains the affinity system; Corruption/Purity is theme +
  the boss gauge, **not** a separate player meta-resource.

> Archangel titles and signature weapons existed in the tabletop lore but were flavor and
> highly asset-dependent — **dropped for now**. The name↔Virtue mapping is enough.

## Paradeisos & the Endgame

The royal court at the tower (kept; most other NPCs are cut):

- **Malek** — The King.
- **Regina** — The Queen, slain by Chaos.
- **Gideon** — Firstborn prince, slain by **Kayne**.
- **Kayne** — the framed pawn, controlled by the Night Mother.
- **Laylah / "The Night Mother"** — advisor to the King; the manipulator behind the throne.
- **Chaos** — **the Source of Corruption**; the final antagonist.
