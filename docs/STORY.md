# The Corrupted Virtues — Story / Campaign Spine

> The **deliberately minimal** campaign structure. Living document — edit freely.
> Scope decision (2026-05-18): for now the campaign is just **"a region, then a boss
> fight,"** repeated. Detailed tabletop beats, side NPCs, and minigames are **cut** until
> the core game feels right. World detail lives in [LORE.md](LORE.md).

## Premise

You are a **Guardian**, a village guardian angel. The seven **Virtues** that rule Areti
are falling to their **Sins**, becoming corrupted **Great Beasts**; their regions decay.
You travel the seven lands, **purge the corruption** from each Virtue (restoring, not
killing them), and finally confront **Chaos**, the Source of Corruption, at Paradeisos.

## Campaign Loop (the whole structure, for now)

For each of the seven regions, repeat this template:

1. **Enter the region** — a corrupted environment (placeholder geometry for now).
2. **Traverse it** — one or more grid tactical battles vs. corrupted minions.
3. **Boss fight** — the corrupted Virtue as a **Great Beast** (2×2 grid unit). Win by
   depleting its **Corruption gauge** (purify, don't kill).
4. **Region restored** — the Virtue is freed, the region heals, and the player
   progresses to the next region.

That's it. No towns, escort quests, branching allegiances, or minigames yet — those are
parked until the core loop is proven fun.

## The Seven Regions (order TBD, content placeholder)

Enkrateia (Temperance/Nature) · Katharot (Purity/Water) · Filanthropia (Charity/Earth) ·
Epimony (Patience/Fire) · Kalosine (Kindness/Energy) · Epimaleia (Diligence/Darkness) ·
Tapenoteia (Humility/Light). _Per-region creature/boss design: see [LORE.md](LORE.md) —
original creatures fitting each Virtue's vibe, not the D&D placeholders._

## Finale — Paradeisos

The tower and its royal-court tragedy: King **Malek**, the slain Queen **Regina** and
Prince **Gideon**, **Kayne** the framed pawn, **Laylah / the Night Mother** as the
manipulator, and **Chaos**, the Source of Corruption — the final confrontation.

## Cast (kept intentionally small)

- **The Choir** — seven Archangels, one per Virtue. Full roster in [LORE.md](LORE.md).
- **The Royal Court** — Malek, Regina, Gideon, Kayne, Laylah/Night Mother, Chaos.

_All other tabletop NPCs (pirate crews, town folk, merchants, etc.) are cut for now._
